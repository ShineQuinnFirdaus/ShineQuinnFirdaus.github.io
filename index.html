ABOUT US

Welcome to an unforgettable adventure in Labuan Bajo! Get ready to explore spectacular natural beauty and experience true comfort with our standard phinisi boat trip. Labuan Bajo is the gateway to Indonesia's incredible natural wonders, particularly the famous Komodo National Park. By phinisi boat, you will have the opportunity to explore incredible places such as Komodo Island, Rinca Island, Padar Island, and Pink Beach. On this journey, you will experience the warmth of the tropical sun, the beauty of the underwater, as well as the unforgettable charm of nature.
Our boats are specially designed to meet your needs and comfort during your trip. Made of beautiful wood, these phinisi boats offer exceptional facilities. There are private cabins equipped with bathrooms, air conditioning, and comfortable beds to ensure you feel refreshed and relaxed every day. Our phinisi boats are also equipped with spacious common spaces, including dining and lounge areas, which allow you to enjoy delicious dishes prepared by our talented chefs and relax in a cozy environment. There is also a spacious upper deck, where you can enjoy the sun and spectacular views along the way.

DESTINATION

Kalong Island

When visiting there, you will see many exotic trees that are a great habitat for millions of giant bats or bats. That's why the island is named Kalong Island. This uninhabited island is one of the attractions that began to steal the attention of tourists because it has one of the most beautiful sunset views in Komodo National Park.

Kelor Island

With its soft and clean sand and crystal clear water without corals in it, visitors feel at home spending time playing water on this island. The underwater scenery when snorkeling is no less beautiful. We can see coral reefs with a variety of colorful and very cute fish around them.

Padar Island

The uniqueness of this island is its stunning natural scenery, especially from the top of the hill. From the top, we can see three beaches with different colors of sand, namely white, black and red. In addition, on the other side of the hill, we can also see a very blue ocean.

Pink Beach

Pink Beach is one of the beaches on Komodo Island, East Nusa Tenggara. This beach is called Pink Beach because of its pink sand. Pink Beach there are so many marine biota including 1000 species of fish, 260 species of coral reefs, and 70 species of sponges.

Kanawa Island

Located in the middle of the waters of East Nusa Tenggara (NTT), the island has become a hidden paradise for nature lovers hungry for exotic adventures, incredible diving experiences, and peacefulness that is hard to find anywhere else. At 1.5 kilometers long and 200 meters wide, Kanawa Island is a gem waiting to be explored by adventurers from all corners of the globe..

Komodo Island

Komodo Island is one of the amazing wonders of the world. Komodo Island is located in the Nusa Tenggara Islands. Komodo Island is known as the original habitat of Komodo dragons. The island is also home to the Komodo National Park. In addition to Komodo dragons, the island also holds a variety of exotic flora.
